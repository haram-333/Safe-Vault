What is asset verification?

Absolutely. We use a non-custodial approach, meaning your private keys never leave your device. We only read public blockchain data to verify balances.

Which wallets are supported?

We support MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and other Web3-compatible wallets that connect to Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain networks.

How long does verification take?

Verification is instant! Once you connect your wallet and submit the amount, our system immediately checks your balance on the blockchain.
